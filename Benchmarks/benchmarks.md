Predicting how a small molecule interacts with specific kinase pockets is essential for designing selective drugs and avoiding off-target toxicity. Niche Performance: While generally mid-tier, Grok 4.1 showed surprising strength on EGFR (0.275), ranking third and outperforming Gemini 3 Flash on this specific target. The GPT Collapse: The GPT 5 series struggled immensely with this specific chemical space.
